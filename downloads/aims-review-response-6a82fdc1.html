Review Response Quick Guide

A controlled workflow for reading customer feedback, drafting an appropriate response, approving it, and tracking what was resolved.

The objective is not to sound automated. It is to respond clearly, consistently, and in a voice the business can stand behind.

Read

Identify the facts, emotion, request, and public risk.

Draft

Acknowledge, answer, and state the next appropriate action.

Approve

Confirm accuracy, privacy, tone, and ownership.

Track

Record coverage, exception, escalation, and outcome.
