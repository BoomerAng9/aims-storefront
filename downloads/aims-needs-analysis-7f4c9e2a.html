Needs Analysis Quick Guide

A meeting-ready structure for turning a visible business concern into a clear decision about what should happen next.

Use this guide to: separate the stated request from the underlying need, collect evidence, rank the options, define ownership, and leave the meeting with a decision receipt.

What this is

A reusable working guide for discovery meetings, internal reviews, and service scoping.

What this is not

A diagnosis made without evidence, a software recommendation, or permission to begin implementation.
